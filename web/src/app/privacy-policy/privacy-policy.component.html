Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

  • Our service prioritizes user privacy, including administrators.
  • This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect the information of both general users
  • and administrators when using our service.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our service, we may collect the following information:
  • Account Information: Username, email
  • Transaction Information: History of points accumulation and redemption
  • Device Information: Device number, operating system, browser type
  • Location Information: To provide location-based services
  • Administrator Information: Access level, activity logs, and system management data

3. How We Use Information

We use your information to:
  • Manage accounts and provide points accumulation services
  • Enhance user experience in the app
  • Prevent fraud and improve security
  • Analyze data to develop features and improve app performance
  • Log and monitor administrator activities to prevent misuse

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, use, or disclosure of data, including:
  • Data encryption
  • Restricting data access to authorized personnel only
  • Data backups to prevent loss
  • Monitoring administrator activities to prevent unauthorized access

5. User and Administrator Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data, including:
  • Requesting a copy of your personal data
  • Correcting inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Requesting deletion of your personal data
  • Withdrawing consent for data usage in certain cases
  • Administrators can access and manage system data according to their granted permissions

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

  • The app may use cookies or other tracking technologies to enhance user experience
  • You can manage or delete cookies through your device or browser settings

7. Policy Changes

  • We may update this policy from time to time. Please check for changes on our app.
  • If there are significant changes, we will notify you in advance.
